► ASSESSMENT APPEALS BOARD
The Assessment Appeals Board hears appeals from property owners who dispute the assessed value of their property as determined by the County Assessor.

Appeals filing period: July 2 – November 30 each year
Board Clerk: (555) 800-9910

Note: This office does not publish assessor contact information. For Assessor's Office contact, see the Elected Officials directory.
