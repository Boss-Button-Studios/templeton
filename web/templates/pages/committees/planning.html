► ABOUT THE PLANNING COMMISSION
The Templeton County Planning Commission is a five-member advisory body appointed by the Board of Supervisors. The Commission reviews land use applications, general plan amendments, subdivision maps, and zoning changes.

Regular meetings are held on the third Thursday of each month at 6:00 PM in the County Administration Building, Room 204. 		► Commission Resources
