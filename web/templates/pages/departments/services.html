►  DEPARTMENT OVERVIEW
The Community Services Department provides a wide range of services to Templeton County residents, including recreation programs, senior services, housing assistance, and community outreach.

Our department publishes a comprehensive County Services Directory listing all available programs and contact information. The directory is updated periodically and is available for download from this page. 		► Directory & Publications
